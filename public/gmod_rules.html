KappaGaming TTT Rules

Rules

    General Rules

  1. No cheating or hacking.
  2. Do not RDM on this server. This means do not randomly shoot, kill, or hurt another player.
  3. No ghosting, do not give information about the current match to another player in the game through an external piece of communication software such as Skype or Teamspeak.
  4. Mic spamming and/or text spamming will result in a gag/mute of the player.
  5. Do not propkill or proppush.
  6. Do not propsurf.
  7. Be respectful of other players and server staff.
  8. No graphic sprays.
  9. No unreadable usernames.
  10. Do not try to delay the current match in any way.
  11. No scamming.
  12. No killing of other players by using props while you are in spectator mode.

    Innocent/Detective Rules

  1. No cheating or hacking.
  2. Do not RDM.
  3. No random calling KOS.

Clarifications

    RDM

  • Randomly shooting and/or hurting other players.
  • Killing someone based on suspision.
  • Killing another player if they are following you.
  • Prop damaging and/or prop killing.
  • Commiting Traitorous Acts if you are not one.

    Traitorous Acts

  • Shooting at/around another player
  • Throwing grenades randomly or into a group of people.
  • Not complying with the detective.
  • Not identifying bodies.
  • Randomly kalling KOS on other players.
  • Planting C4.

    Ghosting

  • You are not allowed to give information about the game to anothe player in the match.
  • No using external forms of communication.
  • For example, you can't say who is the traitor to your friend in the same match through Teamspeak or Skype.
  • You also must not give information away about your role.

Punishment

    RDM

  • 1st offence: Verbal warning
  • 2nd offence: Slay
  • 3rd offence: 1 day ban
  • 4th offence: 3 day ban
  • 5th offence: 5 day ban

    RDM and Leave

  • 1st offence: 1 day ban
  • 2nd offence: 1 week ban
  • 3rd offence: 2 week ban
  • 4th offence: 3 week ban
  • 5th offence: 5 week ban

    Spamming

  • 1st offence: Gag/mute
  • 2nd offence: kick
  • 3rd offence: 1 day ban
  • 4th offence: 3 day ban
  • 5th offence: 5 day ban
